The tale of Two Cities

Charles Dickens

I was in fifth grade when I started reading the classics like Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea, Moby Dick, The Last of the Mohicans and The Tale of Two Cities.

Nothing has effected me more than The Tale of Two Cities.
